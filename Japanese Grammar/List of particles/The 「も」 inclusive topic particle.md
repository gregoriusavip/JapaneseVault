- The topic particle with the additional meaning of “**also**”. It can introduce another topic in addition to the current topic.
- [[#^624682|The inclusion of 「も」 must be consistent with the answer.]]
- [[#^5c8ac9|If it's not consistent, use the 「は」 particle to make a break from the inclusion]]
- [[#^81e94e|Example of inclusion with the negative]]
## Example 1

ボブ：アリスは学生？  
Bob: Is Alice (you) student?

アリス：うん、トム**も**学生。  
Alice: Yeah, and Tom is _also_ student. ^624682
## Example 2

ボブ：アリスは学生？  
Bob: Is Alice (you) student?

アリス：うん、でもトム**は**学生じゃない。  
Alice: Yeah, but Tom is not student. ^5c8ac9
## Example 3

ボブ：アリスは学生？  
Bob: Is Alice (you) student?

アリス：ううん、トム**も**学生じゃない。  
Alice: No, and Tom is also not student. ^81e94e
