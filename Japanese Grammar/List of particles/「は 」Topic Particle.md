- Identifies what it is that you’re talking about, essentially the topic of your sentence
- Pronounced as /wa/
- [[#^937259|Once topic is established, the responder don't need to repeat the topic]]
- [[#^5d1bb3|Without context the meaning sometimes couldn't be extracted]]
- [[#^671528|Topic can be referring to any action or object from anywhere even including other sentences]]
## Example 1

ボブ：アリス**は**学生？  
Bob: Is Alice (you) student?

アリス：うん、学生。  
Alice: Yeah, (I) am. ^937259
## Example 2

ボブ：ジョン**は**明日？  
Bob: John is tomorrow? ^5d1bb3

アリス：ううん、明日じゃない。  
Alice: No, not tomorrow.
## Example 3

アリス：今日は試験だ。  
Alice: Today is exam.

ボブ：ジョンは？  
Bob: What about John?

アリス：ジョンは明日。  
Alice: John is tomorrow. (As for John, the exam is tomorrow.) ^671528
